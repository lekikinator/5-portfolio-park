Who I am

I am an aspiring 16-year-old writer with dreams to create films and/or books that can move people and change their outlook on the world. I have many interests, from astrophysics to baking. My favorite color is yellow; I associate it with many happy memories. I love Cheetos and MCU films, as well as warm, fuzzy things. I am an unfaithful follower of the Oxford comma and am afraid of seedless watermelons and the deep sea. My religions include Christianity and pouring milk before cereal.
